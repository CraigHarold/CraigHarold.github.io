EURO KONKRETE BUILDING DYNAMICS LLC.

Euro Konkrete prides itself on the diverse portfolio of waterproofing solutions, providing its customers with the most competitive price while maintaining high quality and reliability that they all come to expect

Euro Konkrete Building Dynamics LLC., is an offshoot of Pudlo Middle East Building Materials LLC., (PME) which was part of the DB (Holdings) Group Limited, UK, after PME decided to shut down their middle east operations effective 30th June 2020, unable to sustain the losses owing to the pandemic and envisaging further losses in the coming months. The entire present team at Euro Konkrete was part of PME right from Regional Director / General Manager Mr. Harold Solomon to all other staff and have retained the same office, warehouse and communication lines as that of Pudlo Middle East. (www.pudlo.ae)

Euro Konkrete are now the authorized promoters of waterproof concrete admixture PUDLO CWP and with this newly floated business entity is poised to take forward the business to retain and capitalize on this well established brand in the UAE present in this part of the region for the past more than 20 years.

  • Who We Are

    PUDLO Middle East Building Materials LLC is solely owned by David Ball Group Ltd, UK. David Ball Group is the manufacturer and distributor of PUDLO CWP waterproof admixture.

    PUDLO is an innovation-led construction material manufacturer at the forefront of the quest for innovative products and construction techniques, committed to developing new products which minimizes the impact on the environment and improve working practices

    Our vision is to be the leading manufacturer of innovative concrete products that will improve the way the construction industry works with a bespoke service and specialist products. Through its knowledge and the passion and commitment of its employees, PUDLO Middle East is able to offer outstanding service and expertise to provide complete solutions to satisfy customer’s needs.

    PUDLO has been used extensively throughout the Middle East for the last 15 years and is trusted as the most durable, waterproof concrete available. Strategically located in Dubai, PUDLO is always ready to provide technical expert advice and assistance when it is needed and fast delivery throughout the Middle East is guaranteed.

  • Pioneering Waterproof Concrete and Durability

    In the 1990s, the performance of integrally waterproof concrete was taken to a new level by David Ball Group ltd as it reformulated PUDLO, the world’s first commercial concrete waterproofing admixture introduced 140 years ago. Re-engineered around modern architectural needs, its enhanced capabilities came with the first – and still unmatched – guarantee of the completed concrete structure.

    From PUDLO’s early use in the pioneering sanitation systems of Victorian London, its success story continues at the cutting edge in projects like London’s Crossrail and the celebrated Dubai Fountains

  • Concrete Innovation is Our Passion

    Research & Development is at the heart of what the Group does, harnessing the power of chemistry to enhance the long-term performance of built structures with a dynamic brand portfolio.

    A major milestone in this sustainable legacy is Cemfree, a totally cement free alternative to conventional concrete and a ground-breaking addition to David Ball Group ltd’s portfolio. With the ability to produce structural concrete without the use of Portland cement, this revolutionary material is the result of years of dedicated development by the Group’s research scientists.

  • Sustainable Development is Our Commitment

    While being the world’s most used building material, modern concrete is not considered as one of the most sustainable solutions. Today’s cement and concrete industries have a consistently impressive record for carbon reduction marshalled by a strong commitment to their environmental responsibilities. By enhancing service life of the building structures, PUDLO provides the real contribution towards environmental capability.

  • Engineered Materials are Our Expertise

    For almost half a century, David Ball Specialist Sands has set the bar in the preparation and supply of British standard and unique, ultra-high quality sands for process testing and manufacturing. Quality that has travelled worldwide to meet the most exacting applications.
